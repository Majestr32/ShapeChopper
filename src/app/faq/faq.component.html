If i play offline and beat record, will it be transfered to the leaderboard?

Yes, it will be transfered to the leaderboard if you connect to the internet again and sign in to google account.

Is it add-free?

Yes, it is.

If i switch my mobile device, will my scores dissapear?

All your records are stored on your phone as well as in the leaderboard, so when you sign in to the google account it can load your record from leaderboard to a new device.
